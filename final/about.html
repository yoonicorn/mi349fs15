ABOUT



Nol-Bu is a Korean barbecue restaurant that serves a range of food and beverage items. The restaurants menu includes Angus prime sirloin steaks, beef, chicken, pork, shrimp and noodle soup. It additionally serves crabs, pan-fried fish, black codfish and prime rib. Nol-Bu features garden-like, open-air dining area to organize private and corporate events. The restaurant is located in Morton Grove.


We are a unique and sumptuous dining experience nestled in the heart of Morton Grove. At Nol-Bu you'll be in for a treat when you taste our famous Korean BBQ made with prime meat cuts. We also have a full menu of authentic Korean dishes featuring vegetarian choices, chicken, shrimp, fish and more.
